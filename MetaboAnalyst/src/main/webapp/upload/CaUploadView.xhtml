Theoretical data set inspired by wolf and elk interactions at Yellowstone National Park, USA. Data consists of counts of wolves and elk (variables/columns) at different sampling times over a 4 year period (samples/rows). Both columns and rows are labeled. Iris flower measurements with species data. Data consists of 4 measurements (petal width, petal length, sepal width, sepal length) and species groupings (variables/columns) for 150 iris flowers (samples/rows). There are 50 flowers from each of 3 species of iris: Iris setosa, Iris versicolor, and Iris virginica. Variables are labeled, but iris flowers are not. (ref.)
